4.3 Title Writing Rules

  • Front-load keywords: Most important keywords in first 80 characters
  • Natural reading: Must sound natural when read aloud
  • Include specs: Voltage, amperage, wattage, dimensions
  • Add use cases: What products is it compatible with?
